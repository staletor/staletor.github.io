My name is Ståle Torger Stokke. I'm born in 1984 and I'm from the beautiful city Trondheim in Norway. I'm an Industrial Designer with a Masters degree in Industrial Design Engineering from The Norwegian University of Technology and Science (NTNU).

Other than my passion for good design I love to boulder and play football. I'm also responsible for the Designmagnet group in Trondheim. When I'm not active with some sports or Designmagnet you can find me at a concert or hanging out with my friends. Well not all the time, even I need to rest a couple of hours each day.


Design Process

I believe in an iterative design process with user involvement. I see user involvement as a mean to ensure quality and as a tool for inspiration.

Product Design

Product design was my main focus during my studies. I love to create with my hands and explore the possibilities within a material.

Interaction Design

The last couple of years I've been working with interaction design for a complex technological use case. Making technology affordable through GUI is an exciting challenge.
